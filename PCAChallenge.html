PCA Challenge

PCA Challenge is a driving game inspired by the grassroots motorsport of Autotesting. The Objective is to set the fastest time around a predetermined course. There are obsticles on the the course that the player has to navigate succesfully. Obstacles spawn at predetermined locations but the obstical is chosen at random. If the player fails to do the course correctly they get penalised, and likewise if they hit a cone.
